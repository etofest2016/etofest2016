A. Sekatsky “Metaphysics & Animation”. 11 september

September

Sunday

Assembly Hall of the Botanical Garden. St.Petersburg, Prof. Popov street 2. Metro Petrogradskaya; map

Registration (free of charge)      https://eto-fest.timepad.ru/event/371842/

Metaphysics always appeals to (in)direct inclusions of sub-semantic layers for the argumentations where for understanding and acceptance of terms self-explorer shall be equipped with metaphysical Hearing and Feeling as well as intuition itself. A. Sekatsky will make a step to cover the theme and touch the point of coincidence of animation and metaphysics.

The workshop will be supported with visual (animation) materials:

Chulyen, a crow's tale - Cerise Lopez

Limbo-Limbo Travel - Zsuzsanna Kreif&Borbála Zétényi

Aleksandr Sekatsky is a Senior Lecturer in Social philosophy and philosophy of history at SPBGU, candidate of philosophical sciences. Research interests: metaphysics of Lie, a problem of historical time, philosophical spyology, philosophy of temptation, history of sensivity.
